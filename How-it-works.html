How PawTrax Works

Experience peace of mind with our advanced pet tracking technology

FAST ● RELIABLE ● ACCURATE

Advanced GPS Technology

Pawtrax connects to multiple satellites for precise pet location tracking. Unlike other trackers, Pawtrax leverages true 4G mobile technology to swiftly and reliably send GPS updates directly to your phone app.

Unmatched Coverage

Equipped with a universal multi-network SIM card, Pawtrax offers extensive coverage where other trackers might lose connection.

Advanced GPS technology ensures precise location tracking

Continuous Tracking Modes

Stay connected with your pet wherever they go.

Find Mode

Instantly activates live tracking, updating your pet's location every five seconds for quick retrieval when you need it most.

Regular Mode

Sends location updates every 30 seconds to two hours, adjusting based on your pet's activity to conserve battery while keeping you informed.

Comprehensive health insights at your fingertips

Activity Data Insights

Pawtrax analyzes your pet's profile (breed, weight, age) along with daily step counts to calculate calories burned, distance traveled, and overall activity levels.

Health Trends

Review daily, weekly, and monthly trends to gain valuable insights into your pet's health and activity patterns.

Safety Zones

Protect your pet with Pawtrax Safety Zones, which create a virtual fence around your home or any designated area.

Instant Notifications

By syncing your WiFi, you receive timely notifications if your pet strays outside the safe zone, allowing for quick action.

Customizable Areas

Create multiple safe zones for your home, park, or other frequent locations for comprehensive protection.

Safety zones keep your pet within designated areas

Long-lasting battery ensures constant protection

Smart Battery Management

Pawtrax trackers are equipped with long-lasting batteries, designed to keep you focused on your pet rather than on charging.

Efficient Power Usage

Smart algorithms adjust location update frequency based on your pet's activity, ensuring efficient battery use.

Long Battery Life

Enjoy weeks of continuous use on a single charge, with low-battery notifications so you're never caught off guard.
