PawTrax Help Center

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is very simple:

  1. Click the "Login/Register" button at the top right of the website
  2. Select the "Register" option
  3. Enter your email and set a password
  4. Click the "Register" button to complete

After registration, you can enjoy a more convenient shopping experience and member-exclusive benefits.

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, follow these steps to reset it:

  1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
  2. Enter the email address you used to register
  3. Check your email and click the reset link you receive
  4. Set a new password and confirm

If you don't receive the email, please check your spam folder or contact customer service.

What is your privacy policy?

We take your privacy very seriously. Our privacy policy includes:

  • We only collect necessary user information
  • We will not share your personal information without permission
  • We use encryption technology to protect data transmission
  • You can request to delete your account data at any time

The complete privacy policy can be viewed via the link at the bottom of the website.

How can I check my order status?

You can check your order status in the following ways:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to the "My Orders" page
  3. Find the corresponding order to view detailed status

Non-logged-in users can check using the order number and registered email on the "Order Inquiry" page.

What should I do if my payment fails?

If you encounter payment failure, please try:

  1. Check your bank card balance or credit limit
  2. Confirm the payment information was entered correctly
  3. Try a different payment method
  4. Wait 15 minutes and try again

If the problem persists, please contact our customer service and provide the order number for troubleshooting.

Can I modify an order after it's been placed?

Orders can be modified within 30 minutes after successful payment:

  1. Go to "My Orders"
  2. Click "Request Modification"
  3. Select the items you want to modify
  4. Submit the modification request

Orders that exceed 30 minutes or have already shipped cannot be modified. You may choose to return the item and place a new order.

How long does delivery take?

Our standard delivery times are:

  • Domestic orders: 3-5 business days
  • International orders: 7-14 business days

Specific delivery times may vary depending on product availability and shipping address. Estimated delivery times can be viewed on the checkout page.

How can I track my order?

You can track your order in the following ways:

  1. Log in to your account and go to "My Orders"
  2. Click the "View Logistics" button for your order
  3. The system will display the latest logistics information

You will also receive an email notification with a tracking link after your order ships.

What are the shipping fees?

Our shipping fee standards are as follows:

  • Orders over $299: Free shipping
  • Standard shipping: $10
  • Express shipping: $25 (next-day delivery)
  • Remote areas: Additional $15 charge

Specific fees will be automatically calculated and displayed during checkout.

How do I request a return?

The return process is as follows:

  1. Log in to your account and go to "My Orders"
  2. Select the order you want to return
  3. Click "Request Return" and provide a reason
  4. Wait for customer service approval (within 1 business day)
  5. Return the product as instructed after approval is granted

After we receive and inspect the returned item, we will issue a refund within 3-5 business days.

What is the return period?

Our return policy is:

  • Unused products: Can be returned within 7 days of receipt
  • Products with quality issues: Can be returned within 15 days of receipt
  • Customized products: Cannot be returned

Please keep the original packaging and tags intact when returning items.

How long do refunds take?

Refund processing times are as follows:

  • Credit/Debit cards: 3-5 business days
  • Alipay/WeChat Pay: 1-3 business days
  • Bank transfer: 5-7 business days

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method. The exact time it takes to receive the refund depends on your bank's processing speed.

How does the GPS collar work?

Our GPS tracking collar uses multiple positioning technologies:

  1. GPS Positioning: Obtains precise location via satellite (5-10 meter accuracy)
  2. LBS Positioning: Uses mobile network positioning when GPS signal is weak
  3. Wi-Fi Positioning: Improves accuracy in indoor environments
  4. Motion Sensor: Records pet activity levels and behavior patterns

Data is transmitted to your mobile app in real time via 4G network.

What is the location update frequency?

You can set different update frequencies as needed:

  • Real-time Mode: Updates every 10 seconds (higher battery consumption)
  • Standard Mode: Updates every 1 minute (recommended for daily use)
  • Power-saving Mode: Updates every 5 minutes (suitable when battery is low)
  • Sleep Mode: Updates every hour (for nighttime use)
What is the collar's waterproof rating?

Our collar has an IP67 waterproof rating:

  • Can withstand immersion in 1 meter of water for 30 minutes
  • Rainproof and splash-proof
  • Suitable for pet baths and swimming
  • Not recommended for prolonged underwater use

After use, please dry the charging port area.

How long does the battery last when fully charged?

Battery life depends on usage mode:

  • Real-time Mode: ~12 hours
  • Standard Mode: ~2-3 days
  • Power-saving Mode: ~5-7 days
  • Sleep Mode: ~10-15 days

The 800mAh lithium battery takes about 2 hours to fully charge.

How will I know when the battery is low?

You'll be notified of low battery in these ways:

  1. The app will push a notification when battery falls below 20%
  2. The collar's LED indicator will flash orange
  3. The web portal will show remaining battery percentage
  4. When battery falls below 10%, update frequency automatically adjusts to power-saving mode

We recommend charging when battery falls below 30%.

What should I pay attention to when charging?

Charing precautions:

  • Use the included magnetic charging cable
  • Ensure the charging port is dry and clean
  • LED light shows red while charging, turns green when fully charged
  • For first use, we recommend charging for 4 hours
  • Avoid overcharging (more than 8 hours)
  • For battery health, we recommend fully discharging and recharging at least once per month
How do I set up a safe zone (geofence)?

To set up a geofence:

  1. Open the app and go to "Safe Zone" settings
  2. Draw the safe area on the map (circle or polygon)
  3. Set the fence radius (minimum 100 meters)
  4. Select alert method (app notification/SMS/phone call)
  5. Save settings

You'll receive an immediate alert when your pet leaves the safe zone.

Can I view historical activity routes?

Yes, you can view detailed history:

  • Route Playback: View activity routes for any time period
  • Activity Stats: Daily distance walked, active hours
  • Behavior Analysis: Resting, walking, running time ratios
  • Location Marking: Add notes to important locations

Data is stored for up to 90 days (1 year for VIP members).

Can one collar track multiple pets?

Currently, multi-pet tracking isn't supported:

  • One collar corresponds to one pet profile
  • But one account can manage multiple collars
  • Easily switch between pets in the app
  • Family members can share viewing permissions

To track multiple pets, you'll need to purchase additional collars.

What should I do if the collar is lost?

If the collar is lost:

  1. Immediately mark it as lost in the app
  2. Check the last known location and go look for it
  3. Enable the buzzer function (if available)
  4. Contact customer service to freeze the device
  5. You can purchase device recovery insurance

We recommend always securing the collar properly and using anti-loss accessories.

Is there an additional fee for the SIM card?

SIM card service includes:

  • First year of data service is free (included in device price)
  • Starting the second year, you can choose a renewal plan:
    • Basic: $60/year (10MB daily data)
    • Standard: $120/year (30MB daily data)
    • Premium: $200/year (unlimited data)

Data is mainly used for location transmission and doesn't include call functions.

What should I do if the location is inaccurate?

If experiencing inaccurate location:

  1. Check if you're in an open area (GPS needs sky visibility)
  2. Confirm the device has sufficient battery
  3. Try manually refreshing the location
  4. Restart the device
  5. Check if the app is up to date

If the problem persists, contact customer service and provide:

  • Specific time and location of inaccuracy
  • Device ID and software version number
  • Screenshots or description of the issue
