Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Pawtrax Standard

How does the audio recognition feature work?

Pawtrax Standard uses advanced audio sensors to detect unusual sounds like barking, whining, growling, or signs of distress. When it detects potentially concerning sounds, it sends an immediate alert to your smartphone. The system is trained to distinguish between normal pet sounds and those indicating possible danger or distress.

How accurate is the temperature monitoring?

The temperature sensor is accurate to ±0.5°C in the range of -10°C to 50°C. It samples the ambient temperature every 5 minutes and alerts you immediately if the temperature exceeds the safe range (-10°C to 40°C) that we've determined for pet safety. This helps protect your pet from heatstroke in summer and hypothermia in winter.

What makes the Pawtrax Standard better than the Lite version?

Pawtrax Standard includes all the features of Lite plus several significant upgrades: extended battery life (up to 8 days vs 3 days), enhanced audio recognition capabilities, temperature monitoring, improved waterproofing (IP68 vs IP67), Wi-Fi connectivity, and the unique Pawtrax Sound feature. It's designed for pet owners who want the highest level of monitoring and protection.

Is the larger size suitable for cats?

While Pawtrax Standard is designed primarily for dogs weighing over 3.5kg, it can also be used for larger cats (over 5kg). For smaller cats, we recommend our Lite model which is more compact. The Standard model offers superior features but at a slightly larger size and weight.

How does Pawtrax Sound work?

Pawtrax Sound emits a unique high-frequency tone (inaudible to humans) that your pet can be trained to recognize. When you activate it through the app, the tracker plays this sound which can help your pet find their way back to you or make them more responsive to your location. With consistent training, pets learn to associate this sound with returning home.
