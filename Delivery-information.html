Shipping From Mainland China

We currently ship worldwide from our warehouses in Mainland China. For special delivery requests or unsupported regions, please contact support@pawtrax.com.

Processing Time

  • Orders placed before 2pm CST are processed same day (Mon-Fri, excluding Chinese public holidays)
  • Weekends/holiday orders resume processing the next business day

Important Shipping Notes

  • Deliveries exclude weekends and public holidays in both China and the destination country
  • Customs fees (if applicable) are the customer's responsibility (except for EU orders with prepaid VAT)
  • Tracking emails with carrier links are sent upon dispatch
  • Signature policies vary by carrier (e.g., DHL requires direct signature; local post offices may hold parcels if unattended)
  • Delivery delays may occur during peak seasons (Chinese New Year, Singles Day, etc.)
